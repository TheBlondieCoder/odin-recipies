Don't Miss this! Chocolate-Cherry Fluff.

    Ingredients:


  • 4 Medium containers of cool whip-or 2 large
  • 2 Boxes of chocolate Pudding
  • 3 jars of marachino cherries
  • 2 bags of Marshmellows

    Baking Instructions


  1. Put all of the cool whip in a large bowl

  2. Open the pudding boxes and put the chocolate pudding powder into the cool whip

  3. Put 2 bags of Marshmellow bags into the cool whip bowl

  4. Take the cherries and drain them. Keep the cherry juice.

  5. Put the now drained cherries into the cool whip bowl

  6. Take a rubber Spatula and gently stir the Fluff until it is completely mixed.! Be careful not to over stir
